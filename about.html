About

Heya, I'm Lon!

I'm a 2D animator specializing in anime and action animation. I love to improve, I strive for excellency and to create exciting animations, that brings a smile to peoples faces.

I am currently attending The Animation Workshop in Denmark and will graduate with a bachelor in Character Animation in 2026. I have experience freelancing from 2019-2023 as an illustrator and have now pivoted to freelancing for animation. Next to indie projects, I had the honor of working on One Piece for Toei Animation and have been working on several more, as of yet unannounced projects in the Anime Industry.

I am excited to work with you!
